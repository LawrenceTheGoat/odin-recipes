Lasagna

Description

Lasagna recipe. Meat chunks and bolognese sauce interwoven between layers of pasta.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cheese (mozzarella and parmesan)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Herbs (basil, oregano)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and garlic until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up, finishing with cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
